Home page

Sometimes, life can feel like it's too much, and we don't always know where to turn for help. It’s okay to not have all the answers, and it’s okay to seek support. Being human can be tough, and we all experience moments when it feels like the weight of the world is on our shoulders.

About me

Hi there! I'm Nelli, I am from Hungary originally and I am a mom of three. I will be a future therapist dedicated to helping individuals and families navigate life’s challenges and find a path to healing. With a certificate in family relations, I’ve had the privilege of studying child development, parenting, and the many ways in which our relationships shape our lives. My journey in the mental health field is just beginning, but I am already passionate about creating a safe space for people to explore their feelings, develop coping strategies, and grow into the best versions of themselves.

Resume and Portfolio

Currently, I am working toward my BS degree in Family and Human Services, and I plan to continue my education with a Master's in Counseling. My goal is to empower others to move through life's difficulties, whether that’s managing stress, building better relationships, or overcoming personal challenges. Through my studies and practical experience, I’m learning how to help people of all ages navigate their emotions and the complexities of their personal lives. I believe that everyone deserves the chance to live a fulfilling and balanced life, and I’m committed to walking alongside you as you work toward emotional well-being, healing, and growth.
